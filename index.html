What is an IP?

“Intellectual Property (IP) is any form of original creation that can be bought or sold,” according to the Intellectual Property Office (IPO). Whether it’s an idea, a design or a product, IP is a valuable asset that can be licensed or sold to generate income. But most people don’t know how IP works and how they can gain from their creations. That’s where we come in.
